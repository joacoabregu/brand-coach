Our AI manifesto
We believe technology should amplify human creativity — never replace it.

At BrandCoach, we use Artificial Intelligence with purpose, ethics, and transparency. AI helps us explore global insights, enhance creativity, and streamline workflows — but every idea, message, and design is reviewed, refined, and approved by humans.

Our commitment is rooted in five guiding principles:
  • Transparency. We make AI involvement visible and traceable in our creative process.
  • Human-first creativity. Technology supports our ideas — it never defines them.
  • Accountability. Our team takes full responsibility for all AI-assisted work.
  • Verification. We fact-check and validate every AI-generated output before use.
  • Continuous learning. We stay informed about emerging tools, ethics, and best practices to evolve responsibly.

What kind of clients do you work with?
We partner with organizations, brands, and leaders across sectors —from Fortune 500 companies to cultural institutions and startups.What they share is a forward-thinking mindset and a desire tocommunicate with clarity, authenticity, and measurable impact.
How do you integrate AI in your process?
We use AI as an intelligent assistant — not a replacement for humancreativity. It helps us analyze data faster, generate insights, andoptimize workflows, allowing our team to focus on what matters most:ideas, empathy, and strategic thinking.
How do projects usually begin?
Every collaboration starts with listening. We begin by understandingyour goals, audience, and context through discovery sessions. Fromthere, we co-design a communication roadmap — balancing strategicclarity, creativity, and technology — before moving intoimplementation and measurement.
How do you measure results?
We define clear KPIs from the start — engagement, perception,reach, or internal alignment — depending on your objectives. Wetrack and adjust continuously, ensuring that every creative decisioncontributes to measurable outcomes.
Where is your team based?
BrandCoach operates as a distributed, multicultural,multigenerational team with collaborators in Latin America, NorthAmerica, and Europe. This global model allows us to stay agile,accessible, and culturally fluent across markets and time zones.
